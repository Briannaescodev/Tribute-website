Monroe Escobar

My name is Brianna Escobar and i chose my little sister as my tribute for today's project. I grew up as an only child for the first 8 years of my life. I was 11 years old when my sister was born. From the moment i first saw (and heard) her i knew she'd be anything but ordinary. My sister is a empathetic, kind, opinated, and bossy burst of energy. She is whole hearted one of my biggest supporters and one of my best friends. Without her and her endless and sometimes demanding support and love, i don't know where i'd be.

My sister is the youngest child and .... a gemini.
